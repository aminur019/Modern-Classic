About Our Material

Our products are made from a variety of neoprene sponge grades in black or cream colour. Our neoprene is sourced from the best Taiwanese and Malaysian plants with whom we have had a working relationship for over 20 years.

  • Std. SBR material weight is approx. 0.25 gram per cm3
  • Average tensile strength
  • Compression set -5% at 5kg/cm2
  • Laminate jersey is mesh polyester in a choice of colours
  • Polyester/Nylon laminated to one or both sides of 2.5mm, 3.5mm or 5mm neoprene rubber outer shell
  • Other laminates including Lycra, velcro, or hyperstretch are also available subject to your brief
  • Neoprene is also available in a number of unlaminated finishes including mesh, rib, sharkskin, and fine mesh.
  • Standard packing by polybag in carton with careful transit packing for certain products

Air freight allow 6-10 days
